Nipple System

Product Overview

Our advanced Nipple Water System provides clean, fresh water delivery to poultry through precision-engineered nipple drinkers that ensure optimal hydration while maintaining superior hygiene standards.

This innovative water delivery system eliminates water wastage, prevents contamination, and reduces labor costs associated with traditional watering methods. Each nipple is designed to provide the perfect water flow rate for different bird ages and sizes, ensuring adequate hydration without spillage.

Manufactured from high-quality, non-toxic materials, our nipple systems are built to withstand continuous use while maintaining consistent performance. The system includes pressure regulators, filters, and easy-maintenance components for reliable operation.

Key Features

This system is designed for supplying water to poultry using nipple drinkers.

  • 22 mm, 15 Feet, 20" Drill Square Pipe – Main water line.
  • Square Couplers – Join two square pipes.
  • Square to Round Couplers – Connects square to round pipes.
  • Nipple Drinkers – Clean water release when birds peck.
  • Teflon Tapes – Seals threads, prevents leakage.
  • Flush Tank Sets – For flushing and cleaning pipeline.
  • View Pipe 20×25 mm – Transparent pipe to monitor water flow.

Purpose: Ensures birds get clean water directly through nipple drinkers, reduces contamination, and saves water.
