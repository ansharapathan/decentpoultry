Grower Cage

Product Overview

Our Grower Cage system is specifically designed for the intermediate growth phase of poultry, providing optimal space and conditions for birds transitioning from chicks to adult layers or broilers.

These spacious cages accommodate the rapid growth and increased activity levels of developing birds while maintaining efficient space utilization. The design incorporates larger mesh spacing, enhanced feeding areas, and improved ventilation systems to support healthy development.

Built with durability in mind, our grower cages feature reinforced construction to withstand the increased activity of growing birds. The modular design allows for easy expansion and reconfiguration as your operation grows.

Available Configurations

Standard Grower Cage

Perfect for 6-16 week old birds. Optimal spacing for healthy growth and development.

Large Grower Cage

Extended space for larger breeds and longer growing periods. Enhanced comfort and mobility.

Multi-Tier System

Maximizes capacity while maintaining bird welfare standards. Ideal for commercial operations.
