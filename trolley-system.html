Trolley System

Product Overview

Our heavy-duty Trolley System provides efficient transportation solutions for poultry farms, designed to handle feed distribution, egg collection, and general farm management tasks with maximum efficiency and minimal labor.

Built with industrial-grade materials and precision engineering, these trolleys are designed to withstand the demanding conditions of commercial poultry operations. The system features smooth-rolling wheels, ergonomic handles, and modular designs that can be customized for specific farm requirements.

Whether you need to transport feed, collect eggs, or move equipment, our trolley systems reduce manual labor, improve operational efficiency, and help maintain consistent farm management routines. The corrosion-resistant construction ensures long-term reliability in various environmental conditions.

Key Features

  • Rooter 3 Tair – Main rooter unit designed for operation
  • G I Bin 200 Kg – Galvanized iron storage bin with 200 kg capacity
  • 8" Wheel Set – Strong wheel set for mobility and support
  • Pulli Set – Pulley system for efficient power transmission
  • Wheel Base – Stable base structure to hold and balance the system
