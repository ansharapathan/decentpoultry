California Layer Cage

Product Overview

Our California Layer Cage system represents the pinnacle of modern poultry housing technology, designed specifically for commercial egg production operations seeking maximum efficiency and bird welfare.

Built with premium galvanized Tata 275GSM GI heavy wire and featuring our proprietary ventilation system, these cages provide optimal living conditions for laying hens while maximizing space utilization and egg collection efficiency. The California design incorporates sloped floors for easy egg rolling, integrated feeding troughs, and advanced nipple drinking systems.

Each cage unit is engineered to house 4-6 birds comfortably, with precise spacing that allows natural movement while preventing aggressive behavior. The modular design enables easy installation and maintenance, making it ideal for both new installations and facility upgrades.

California Cage – Bird Capacity & Box Dimensions

Our California Cage systems are designed with optimal space utilization in mind, ensuring both bird comfort and durability of the structure.

Box Size (Inches) Bird Capacity Description
15" x 20" 5 Birds Each unit is structured to accommodate 5 birds comfortably. The 15-inch depth and 20-inch width ensure that every bird has sufficient movement space while maintaining flock harmony.
15" x 18" 4 Birds This configuration supports 4 birds per box. With slightly reduced width, the design is ideal for farms focusing on compact yet efficient housing without compromising bird welfare.

Both cage variants follow the principles of animal welfare, ventilation, and ease of management, making them suitable for farms of varying scales.
