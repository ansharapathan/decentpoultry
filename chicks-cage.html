Chicks Cage

Product Overview

Our specialized Chicks Cage system is engineered to provide the safest and most nurturing environment for young poultry during their critical early development stages.

Designed with smaller mesh spacing and enhanced safety features, these cages ensure optimal protection for chicks while promoting healthy growth. The system incorporates temperature-controlled environments, specialized feeding mechanisms, and easy-access water systems tailored for young birds.

Each cage unit features adjustable height settings, removable floor panels for easy cleaning, and integrated heating elements for temperature regulation. The design prioritizes chick safety while enabling efficient monitoring and care by farm personnel.

We provide sizes of Chicks Grower System

  • 15×18 Box Size – 15 Bird Chicks, 5 Bird Grower
  • 15×30 Box Size – 20 Bird Chicks, 9 Bird Grower

Key Features

  • Integrated heating system
  • Specialized feeding troughs
  • Easy-access water systems
  • Temperature control features
  • Removable floor panels
  • Adjustable height settings
