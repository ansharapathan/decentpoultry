Feeding System

Product Overview

Our advanced Feeding System revolutionizes poultry nutrition management with automated, precise, and efficient feed distribution technology designed for modern commercial operations.

This comprehensive feeding solution eliminates manual feeding labor while ensuring consistent nutrition delivery to every bird. The system features programmable feeding schedules, portion control mechanisms, and waste reduction technology that maximizes feed efficiency and minimizes operational costs.

Built with food-grade materials and featuring easy-clean designs, our feeding systems maintain the highest hygiene standards while providing reliable, long-term performance in demanding commercial environments.

PVC Feeder System

This system is used for feeding birds (like layers) using PVC pipes and accessories.

  • 18 Feet PVC Layer Feeders (Lotus): Long PVC pipes designed to hold and supply feed for poultry.
  • Layer Feeders PVC Joints (Lotus): Connectors used to join multiple feeder pipes together.
  • Layer Feeder PVC End Caps (Lotus): Caps used at the ends of feeder pipes to prevent spillage and keep feed inside.
  • Layer Feeder PVC Brackets (GLP): Brackets or holders that support the feeder pipes and keep them fixed in place.

Purpose: Provides a durable and hygienic way to distribute feed evenly among birds with minimal wastage.
